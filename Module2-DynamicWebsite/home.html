✨ --- Your Social Hub for Engaging Posts --- ✨

Welcome to ZAKBOOK! A dynamic web application designed to provide users with an interactive platform to explore and engage with posts from various users.

Purpose of ZAKBOOK

ZAKBOOK is a social content hub where users can:
✅ Browse posts with detailed information such as titles, user details, tags, and reactions.
✅ View and interact with comments on each post.
✅ Explore user profiles to learn more about content creators.
✅ Contact us via a simple and well-validated form.

How to Use ZAKBOOK

(1) Navigate Easily - Use the top navigation bar to switch between Home, Posts, and Contact.
(2) Explore Posts - View dynamic content fetched from an API, including post details and user information.
(3) View User Profiles - Click on a username to open a profile modal with user details.
(4) Engage with Comments - Read comments attached to each post for deeper interactions.
(5) Use Pagination or Infinite Scrolling - Seamlessly load more content without interruptions.
(6) Contact Us - Fill out a simple form to get in touch. Ensure your name is valid, your email is correct, and confirm before sending.

ZAKBOOK is built with a focus on user experience, responsiveness, and smooth interactions. Dive in and start exploring today!
